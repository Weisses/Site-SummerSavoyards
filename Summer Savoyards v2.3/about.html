"A warrior without the knowledge of their past history, origin and culture is like a tree without roots."





Ebonheart is a venerable guild created exclusively for the upcoming launch of Crowfall. Battle is the most magnificent competition in which a warrior can engage. It brings out all that is best; purges all that is weak. All warriors are afraid of battle, but a coward is the one who lets his fear overcome his sense of duty.

Though Crowfall has a long way to go before it is live, we plan on creating a guild of like-minded individuals who will focus on enjoying the game and having fun. To be in Ebonheart, one must be friendly and easy going. There is a zero tolerance policy for trolls and anyone who does not uphold the honor of Ebonheart. Let it be known that anyone who disgraces the Order will be exiled.

Ebonheart currently does not have any requirements on recruiting new members other than being active and courteous to allies and enemies alike. Some people forget that these are games we play, and skill will show who is superior. Though we cannot achieve victory every time we battle, we can surely give our opponents a fight they will never forget.


Remember: Don't be bitter, be better.




Battle Lust

We ready our weapons and await our orders. We charge into battle to destroy, and we too are decimated. We can never get enough.


Master Crafters

A mighty kingdom needs skilled men to keep that kingdom standing strong. Many crafters are required for us to maintain self-sufficiency.


Trade and Commerce

Through diplomancy we will prosper. Our Eternal Kingdom will be open for any and all players to freely buy and sell their wares.
